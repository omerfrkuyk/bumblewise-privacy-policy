BumbleWise Demo Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

BumbleWise Demo (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your Instagram or related Meta account, we may receive certain account data such as profile information, username, account type, follower counts, media information, insights, and other data you explicitly authorize through Meta’s permission system.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we receive to:

  • show creator profile statistics and related analytics,

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We only use authorized data for the operation of the app. Data may be processed by service providers or platforms that are necessary for hosting, authentication, analytics, and infrastructure support.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Platforms

Our app may rely on third-party services such as Meta and Instagram. Your use of those services is also subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

6. User Rights

You may request deletion of your connected data or revoke access at any time by disconnecting your account or contacting us.
